Our story

online foodshop, opened its first store in 1999 at Adam’s Arcade in uganda. With the aim of introducing nice cusine culture in Uganda, the first outlet was a coffee shop and later the brand evolved to a Ugandan diner style restaurant to its present-day status as a fast food hub, online food shop is now one of the leading restaurants in Africa and has grown to have outlets in 14 cities across 3 countries in East Africa (Kenya, Uganda and Rwanda).
